Bibliographic Information Hub

A lightweight framework for publishing curated bibliographies

Bibliographic Information Hub (BIH) is a framework for publishing and sharing bibliographic materials by linking multiple identifiers, related resources, and related pages around stable items. It also allows you to curate, structure, and publish bibliographic information yourself when no one else has done so.

Core Concepts

BIH issuer

An issuer is an organization or individual that publishes and maintains BIH collections.

BIH collection

A collection is a published list of bibliographic materials organized around a theme, a holding, or another coherent grouping.

BIH item

An item is the unit that brings together multiple identifiers, related resources and pages, cached bibliographic metadata, and explanatory notes.
